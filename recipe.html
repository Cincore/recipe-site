  • How to make:

  • Step one: Preheat oven to 50 degrees.

  • Step two: Place foil on flat surface and roll out cresent roll.

  • Step three: Spread pizza sauce down the middle of the roll.

  • Step four: Add lots of cheese on top of the pizza sauce.

  • Step five: Add peperoni, or any kind of meat on top of cheese.

  • Step six: Fold the open ends to the middle.

  • Step seven: Put in oven for 20 minutes.

  • Step Eight: WHen finished, let cool, and enjoy.
